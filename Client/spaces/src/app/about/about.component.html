What's Spaces?
Welcome To Spaces For Peace and Development
Spaces for peace and development organization is a non- governmental non – profit nonpolitical national organization founded and registered with the Humanitarian Aid Commission (HAC) in Sudan 2020; and aims on promoting the interest of the local communities to achieve universal and equal access to safe, affordable drinking water (IWRM), quality education, ٌSustainable and resilient cities, clean energy and management of local consumption and production.

Our Vision

Enabling the communities to make a positive change and to be the compass for positive change for those working in the humanitarian field.

Our Mission

We exist to be the first responders to every crisis, providing humanitarian interventions in the best way possible and to sharpen the skills of our community in handling difficult situations through professional training and capacity building.

Our Strategy

Spaces organization strategy to create and enabling environment, enhance local capabilities and strengthen and empower communities and civil society.

Spaces adopted the common humanitarian values
